About Us

Our Cyber Security Community (CSI) was founded within the National Computer Science School in 2010.
Since its creation, it has provided continuous and attractive sessions dedicated to create the awareness that puts security at the top of engineering students minds.

Cryptography

The art and science of protecting information by transforming an encrypted message into a cipher text.

Steganography

The technique of hiding secret data within an ordinary, non secret file or message in order to avoid detection.

Network Security

The network has some vulnerabilities which a user can exploit to gain higher level privilege.

Capture The Flag

A CTF is an information security competition that challenges contestants to solve a variety of tasks.

Hack Me If You Can

A 4-hour CTF competition where problems are based on real-world cases and for almost every level.

Hack Zone Tunisia

A 24-hour event at ENSI to meet with cyber security experts and compete with the best hackers.

Our Activities

We dive into the world of cyber security.
We serve the community.

Content Writing

We share daily posts ("Hack Culture" / "Most known hackers") on our social media to promote cyber security awareness and to engage students in expanding their security knowledge.

Trainings

We provide free, diversified cyber security trainings and learning materials to all students at ENSI. Our trainings include, but not limited to, Cryptography, Steganography, Reverse engineering, Web and networking.

CTF Challenges

We offer the opportunity for students to discover and practice cyber security techniques and concepts throughout a wide range of CTF challenges.

Competitions

We organize CTF competitions to challenge contestants, to develop their teamwork abilities and to sharpen their cybersecurity skills by simulating real-world cases and complex problems.

Collaborations

We build powerful collaborations with other cyber security clubs from different schools to widen our network and to expand our activities.

Meetings

We organize monthly meetings with our members to talk about new security advances and to discuss some cyber security concepts and topics.
