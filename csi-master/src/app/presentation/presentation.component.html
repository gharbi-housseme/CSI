About Us

CSI is a Cybersecurity club based in ENSI, National School of Computer Sciences - Tunisia.
It's an open community for all cybersecurity enthusiasts.
The club's main purpose is to educate students and raise their awareness about cybersecurity within a friendly and interactive atmosphere.

Cryptography

The art and science of protecting information by transforming an encrypted message into a cipher text.

Steganography

The technique of hiding secret data within an ordinary, non secret file or message in order to avoid detection.

Network Security

The network has a lot of vulnerabilities which a user can exploit to gain some kind of higher level privilege.

Capture The Flag

A CTF is a competition between students used as a learning tool that can sharpen their cybersecurity techniques.

Hack Me If You Can

A cybersecurity competition at ENSI, based on 4 hours CTF challenges about real-world cases and complex problems.

Hack Zone Tunisia

One of the greatest cybersecurity events in Tunisia. 24 hours at ENSI to meet with experts and compete with the best hackers.

Our Activities

We dive into the world of cybersecurity.
We serve the community.

Content Writing

We share daily "Hack Culture" posts on our social media to promote cybersecurity awareness and to engage students in expanding their knowledge of cybersecurity and information security.

Trainings

We provide free, diversified cybersecurity trainings and learning materials to all students at ENSI. Our trainings include, but not limited to, Cryptography, Steganography and Network Security.

CTF Challenges

We offer the opportunity for students to practice cybersecurity techniques and concepts throughout a wide range of CTF challenges.

Hackathons

We organize CTF competitions and hackathons to challenge students, to develop their teamwork abilities and to sharpen their cybersecurity skills by simulating real-world cases and complex problems.

Collaborations

We build powerful collaborations with other cybersecurity clubs from different institutions to develop our network and to expand cybersecurity culture.

Meetings

We organize monthly meetings with our members to talk about new security advances and to discuss some cybersecurity concepts and topics.
