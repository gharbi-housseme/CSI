About Hackzone

Tunisia's biggest cyber security event

Hack Zone Tunisia is an event organized by ENSI Information Security Club (CSI ENSI). The event is initially an initiative of the members of the club to sensitize the community towards discipline and to answer certain behaviours and security measures during the use of Internet and IT tools. This service lasts 24 consecutive hours and is divided to a theoretical part organised in the form of conferences and workshops, and a practical part holding challenges on machines where participants use the concepts and notions aquired.
