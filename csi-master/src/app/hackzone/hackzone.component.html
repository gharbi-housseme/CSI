About Hackzone

Among Tunisia's biggest cyber security events

Hack Zone is the flagship event of cyber security community. Organized every year since 2013, Hack Zone has gone beyond the borders of ENSI to become, over the years, an unmissable event in terms of cyber security. After the previous editions success, Hack Zone returns this year in its 8th ONLINE edition being the unmissable meeting of the cyber security curious and passionated people in Tunisia. Students from all over Tunisia will be able to take concrete initiatives and contribute to the establishment of a better digital world. Participants will be able to take advantage of workshops and video conferences presented by high-grade speakers and the most cyber security experts.
