HACK ME IF YOU CAN

about

Our event, which is organized yearly since 2012, aims to strengthen the participants knowledge in cyber security. The program will feature 4 hours of CTF challenges with a coffee break. Problems are based on real-world cases and for almost every level.

why to attend?

we share a mission of spreading the cyber security culture among participants. Hack Me If You Can represents a good opportunity for participants to test their skills in this field, to enhance their problem-solving skills, to gain critical hands on practice and to be well trained for Hack Zone challenges.
