about

Hack me if you can is an annual event focused on helping improve cybersecurity knowledge. The program will feature 4 hours of CTF challenges with a coffee break. Problems are based on real-world cases. Because of the numerous different participators, a range of varied challenges will be ready for almost every level.

why to attend?

we share a mission of spreading the culture of personal web security.This kind of challenges is a great way to learn hacking techniques, strengthen your problem-solving skills, and gain critical hands on practice. This event is organized within the framework of practicing for the challenges of our main event HackZone.

when and where?

The 8th edition will be held onthe 15th of January 2020 at the national school of Computer science (ENSI). We are eager to see you among us on the day of the event. Come and show us your skills. It’s a must-attend event!
