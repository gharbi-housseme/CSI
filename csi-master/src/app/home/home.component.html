Presentation

CSI ENSI is a tunisian Cybersecurity club based in ENSI National School of Computer Sciences - Tunisia. It's an open community for all cybersecurity enthusiasts. Our main purpose is to educate students and raise their awareness about cybersecurity within a friendly and interactive atmosphere. Each year, more than 70 freshmen join the community. The community leaders work hard to ensure new members get acquainted with cyber security, and together, they try to learn through the year the best practices in coding secure applications and programs along with Basic cyber security techniques and principles.

Formation

We provide free, diversified cyber security trainings and learning materials to all students at ENSI. Our trainings include, but not limited to, Cryptography, Steganography and Network Security
